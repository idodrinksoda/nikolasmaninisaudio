About

Who I am

I’m Nikolas, an audio engineer based in Vancouver, BC. With a degree in audio engineering and years of hands-on experience, I take a careful, detail-focused approach to every project — whether that’s a weekly podcast, a voice acting audition demo, or a video that needs a polished edit.

My goal is simple: deliver clean, professional results that match your vision, without over-processing or losing the natural feel of your content.

Services

What I do

🎙

Podcast & Spoken-Word Editing

Clean, consistent audio that keeps your listeners engaged.

  • Noise, hum, and click removal
  • Leveling and dynamic consistency
  • Pacing edits, mistake removal, dead air cuts
  • Platform-ready delivery formats
🎭

Voice Acting Audition Demos

Short-form edits for audition submissions — fast and focused.

  • Tight cleanup and level matching
  • Consistent tone and clarity across reads
  • Fast turnaround for urgent deadlines
🎬

Video Editing

Straightforward video edits — cuts, sync, pacing, and clean exports.

  • Dialogue and B-roll assembly
  • Audio sync and mixing
  • Colour correction and basic visual cleanup
  • Delivery in web, social, or broadcast formats
⚠

Graphics disclaimer: Video editing services cover cuts, assembly, audio work, and basic visual corrections. I do not offer motion graphics, complex animations, VFX, or advanced graphic design beyond simple text titles and basic overlays. If your project requires heavy graphics work, I’m happy to point you toward specialists.
